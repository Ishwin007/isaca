ISACA Training Resources
Source: https://www.isaca.org/training-and-events

ISACA offers a wide range of training options to help professionals prepare for certifications, earn CPE credits, and stay current in fast-moving fields like cybersecurity, IT audit, governance, risk, and privacy.

Types of Training Available

1. Online Self-Paced Courses
•	Available 24/7 — learn at your own pace
•	Covers all major certification domains (CISA, CISM, CRISC, CGEIT, CDPSE)
•	Includes practice questions, videos, and study materials
•	Earns CPE credits upon completion
•	Member discounts available

2. Virtual Instructor-Led Training (VILT)
•	Live online classes led by ISACA-certified instructors
•	Interactive sessions with Q&A and real-world examples
•	Scheduled cohorts throughout the year
•	Ideal for professionals who prefer structured learning with expert guidance

3. In-Person Training
•	Classroom-based training at select locations globally
•	Delivered by authorized ISACA training partners
•	Intensive formats available (3-day, 5-day bootcamps)

4. ISACA Certifications Exam Prep
•	Official exam preparation courses for CISA, CISM, CRISC, CGEIT, and CDPSE
•	Review Manuals and Question, Answer & Explanation (QAE) databases
•	Practice exams that mirror the format of the real exam
•	Available in print and digital formats

5. On-Demand Webinars
•	Free and paid webinars on current topics in cybersecurity, audit, and governance
•	Earn CPE credits by attending live or watching recordings
•	Archived library available to members

6. ISACA Conference and Events
•	Global conferences including GRC Conference, CSX Cybersecurity Conference
•	Chapter events, workshops, and seminars worldwide
•	Networking opportunities with industry peers and leaders

7. Academic and University Programs
•	Partnerships with universities offering ISACA-aligned curricula
•	Student resources including textbooks, case studies, and academic pricing
•	ISACA academic membership for enrolled students

Study Materials Available for Purchase
•	Official Review Manuals (print and digital)
•	Question, Answer & Explanation (QAE) Databases
•	Practice exam bundles
•	Flash cards and study guides

All training and study materials are available at:
www.isaca.org/training-and-events
