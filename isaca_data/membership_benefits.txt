ISACA Membership Benefits
Source: https://www.isaca.org/membership

Joining ISACA connects you to a global community of 185,000+ professionals and gives you access to world-class resources, tools, and opportunities to grow your career in IT audit, security, governance, risk, and privacy.

Membership Fees
•	One-Year Membership: US$135
•	Two-Year Membership: US$255 (save US$15)
•	Student Membership: US$40 (must be enrolled full-time in an accredited institution)

Key Benefits of ISACA Membership

1. Exam Discounts
Members receive significant discounts on all ISACA certification exams:
•	CISA, CISM, CRISC, CGEIT, CDPSE exams: US$575 (vs. US$760 for non-members) — a saving of US$185 per exam

2. Access to Research and Publications
•	Free access to ISACA's Journal (published 6 times a year)
•	Access to frameworks, whitepapers, research reports, and guidelines
•	COBIT and other framework publications at member pricing

3. Online Learning and CPE
•	Discounts on ISACA's online courses and virtual training
•	Access to free CPE-eligible webinars and on-demand content
•	Ability to earn CPE credits to maintain certifications

4. Global Community and Networking
•	Access to ISACA's global network of 185,000+ professionals
•	Membership in one of 225+ local chapters worldwide
•	Opportunities to connect with peers, mentors, and industry leaders

5. Career Resources
•	Access to ISACA's job board and career center
•	Mentoring programs connecting you with experienced professionals
•	Leadership and volunteer opportunities within chapters and committees

6. Knowledge Center
•	Exclusive access to ISACA's online Knowledge Center
•	Templates, tools, and audit programs for practical use
•	Standards, guidelines, and best practice documents

7. Advocacy and Community Involvement
•	Participate in shaping industry standards and policies
•	Contribute to ISACA research and publications
•	Vote in ISACA elections and influence the direction of the organization

How to Join
1.	Visit www.isaca.org/membership
2.	Create or log in to your ISACA account
3.	Select your membership type and duration
4.	Complete payment
5.	Access your member benefits immediately upon joining
