CISA — Certified Information Systems Auditor
Source: https://www.isaca.org/credentialing/cisa

World-renowned as the standard of achievement for auditing, monitoring, and assessing IT and business systems, CISA also acknowledges the importance of emerging technologies. Achieving a CISA certification showcases expertise and the ability to apply a risk-based approach to audit engagements. Addressing innovations like AI and blockchain, CISA ensures IT audit professionals stay current on the latest technology trends.

Certification Requirements
•	Pass the CISA certification exam
•	Pay the US$50 application processing fee
•	Submit application demonstrating required work experience
•	Adhere to the Code of Professional Ethics
•	Adhere to the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) Policy
•	Compliance with Information Systems Auditing Standards
Candidates have five years from passing the exam to apply for certification.

Registration Process
CISA exams are computer-based and administered at authorized PSI testing centers globally or as remotely proctored exams. Registration is continuous — candidates can register any time with no restrictions. Candidates can schedule a testing appointment as early as 48 hours after payment.
1.	Log in to your ISACA Account
2.	Click Certification & CPE Management
3.	Click Schedule Your Exam or Visit Exam Website (PSI dashboard)
4.	On the PSI dashboard, click Schedule Exam

Eligibility
Upon registration, candidates have a six-month eligibility period to take the exam. Exam appointments are available up to 90 days in advance. Rescheduling is permitted without penalty if done at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.
