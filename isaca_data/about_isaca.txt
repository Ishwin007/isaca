About ISACA
Source: https://www.isaca.org/about-us

What is ISACA?
ISACA is a global professional membership organization that supports people who work in IT audit, cybersecurity, governance, risk management, compliance, and privacy. It has been operating for over 50 years and is recognized worldwide as a leading body in these fields.

ISACA helps professionals grow their careers through certifications, training, research, frameworks, and a large global community. It also helps organizations build stronger, more trustworthy technology practices.

Key Facts
•	185,000+ members worldwide
•	Presence in 188 countries
•	231 local chapters across 6 global regions
•	Offices in North America, Europe, and China

What ISACA Offers
•	Globally recognized certifications — CISA, CISM, CRISC, CGEIT, CDPSE, CCOA, and more
•	Training courses, webinars, and conferences
•	Research reports and whitepapers on cybersecurity, governance, and emerging technology
•	Frameworks and standards including COBIT
•	Local chapters for networking and professional development
•	Mentoring programs and career resources
•	Volunteer and leadership opportunities

Who ISACA Serves
ISACA serves individuals and organizations that work with technology and need to manage it securely, responsibly, and in line with regulations. This includes IT auditors, security professionals, risk managers, compliance officers, privacy engineers, and IT governance leaders.

ISACA's Core Values
•	People First — decisions are made in the best interest of members and the community
•	Inclusivity — diverse perspectives are welcomed and barriers to participation are removed
•	Respect — all members and stakeholders are treated with dignity
•	Openness to Change — new ideas are embraced and learning is ongoing
•	Teamwork — the organization works as one unified body
•	Accountability — commitments are honored and actions are transparent

Why ISACA Matters
As technology becomes more central to business and society, the need for professionals who can audit, secure, govern, and manage it responsibly has grown significantly. ISACA provides the credentials and knowledge that help professionals meet this demand and help organizations build trust in their technology.
