AAISM — Advanced in AI Security Management
Source: https://www.isaca.org/credentialing/aaism

The first and only AI-centric security management certification. AAISM is designed for experienced security professionals to manage evolving security risks related to AI, implement policy, and ensure responsible organizational use of AI technologies.

Exam Domains
•	AI Governance and Program Management — managing AI initiatives across the enterprise
•	AI Risk Management — identifying, assessing, monitoring, and mitigating AI-related threats
•	AI Technologies and Controls — understanding AI systems and their security controls

Prerequisites — Who Can Apply?
Candidates must hold one of the following active certifications:
•	CISM (Certified Information Security Manager)
•	CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional)

Note: Candidates should also have proven experience in security or advisory roles with some expertise in assessing, implementing, and maintaining AI systems.

Exam Cost
•	Member: US$459.00
•	Non-Member: US$599.00
•	Application Processing Fee: US$50.00

Registration
Computer-based exams administered at authorized PSI testing centers globally or via remote proctoring. Six-month eligibility period from registration to exam completion. Candidates have five years from passing to apply for certification.

Why Pursue AAISM?
•	Only certification specifically focused on AI security management
•	Validates ability to protect organizations against AI-specific threats
•	Addresses growing demand for security leaders who understand AI risks
•	Complements CISM by adding specialized AI security expertise
•	Demonstrates readiness to advise boards and executives on AI security posture
•	Builds career value as organizations accelerate AI adoption
