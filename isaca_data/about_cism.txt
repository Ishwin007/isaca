CISM — Certified Information Security Manager
Source: https://www.isaca.org/credentialing/cism

CISM is a globally recognized certification for professionals who design, build and manage enterprise information security programs. It validates your ability to manage and govern an organization's information security — going beyond technical skills to focus on strategy and leadership.

Certification Requirements
•	Pass the CISM certification exam
•	Pay the US$50 application processing fee
•	Submit application demonstrating required work experience (5 years in information security management, with at least 3 years in 3 or more domain areas)
•	Adhere to the Code of Professional Ethics
•	Adhere to the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) Policy
Candidates have five years from passing the exam to apply for certification. Substitutions and waivers are available for up to 2 years of work experience.

Registration Process
CISM exams are computer-based and administered at authorized PSI testing centers globally or as remotely proctored exams. Registration is continuous — candidates can register any time with no restrictions. Candidates can schedule a testing appointment as early as 48 hours after payment.
1.	Log in to your ISACA Account
2.	Click Certification & CPE Management
3.	Click Schedule Your Exam or Visit Exam Website (PSI dashboard)
4.	On the PSI dashboard, click Schedule Exam

Eligibility
Upon registration, candidates have a six-month eligibility period to take the exam. Exam appointments are available up to 90 days in advance. Rescheduling is permitted without penalty if done at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.
