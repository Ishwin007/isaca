CDPSE — Certified Data Privacy Solutions Engineer
Source: https://www.isaca.org/credentialing/cdpse

The Certified Data Privacy Solutions Engineer (CDPSE) validates technical expertise in implementing privacy-by-design principles into existing and future systems. CDPSE holders work across departments to develop ethical, human-centered privacy solutions in a complex regulatory environment.

Certification Requirements
•	Pass the CDPSE certification exam
•	Pay the US$50 application processing fee
•	Submit application demonstrating required work experience
•	Adhere to the Code of Professional Ethics
•	Adhere to the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) Policy
Candidates have five years from passing the exam to apply for certification.

•	Member: US$575.00

Registration Process
CDPSE exams are computer-based and administered at authorized PSI testing centers globally or as remotely proctored exams. Registration is continuous — candidates can register any time with no restrictions. Candidates can schedule a testing appointment as early as 48 hours after payment.
1.	Log in to your ISACA Account
2.	Click Certification & CPE Management
3.	Click Schedule Your Exam or Visit Exam Website (PSI dashboard)
4.	On the PSI dashboard, click Schedule Exam

Eligibility
Upon registration, candidates have a six-month eligibility period to take the exam. Rescheduling is permitted without penalty if done at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.

Who Should Pursue CDPSE?
•	Privacy engineers and architects
•	Data protection officers (DPOs)
•	IT professionals implementing GDPR or other privacy regulations
•	Developers building privacy-compliant systems
•	Security professionals expanding into privacy

Why Certify with CDPSE?
•	Only certification focused on the technical implementation of privacy
•	Demonstrates ability to embed privacy into systems from the ground up
•	Highly valued in industries handling personal data — healthcare, finance, retail, tech
•	Validates knowledge of GDPR, CCPA, and other global privacy regulations
•	ANAB accredited for internationally recognized credibility
