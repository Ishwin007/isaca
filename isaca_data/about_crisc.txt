CRISC is the only certification that prepares and enables IT professionals to become strategic partners to the business by helping them understand business risk and how IT controls can mitigate that risk. It is the most current and rigorous assessment available for risk management professionals.

Certification Requirements
•	Pass the CRISC certification exam
•	Pay the US$50 application processing fee
•	Submit application demonstrating required work experience (3 years of cumulative work experience in at least 2 of the 4 CRISC domains, including Domain 1 or 2)
•	Adhere to the Code of Professional Ethics
•	Adhere to the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) Policy
Candidates have five years from passing the exam to apply for certification.

Registration Process
CRISC exams are computer-based and administered at authorized PSI testing centers globally or as remotely proctored exams. Registration is continuous — candidates can register any time with no restrictions. Candidates can schedule a testing appointment as early as 48 hours after payment.
1.	Log in to your ISACA Account
2.	Click Certification & CPE Management
3.	Click Schedule Your Exam or Visit Exam Website (PSI dashboard)
4.	On the PSI dashboard, click Schedule Exam

Eligibility
Upon registration, candidates have a six-month eligibility period to take the exam. Exam appointments are available up to 90 days in advance. Rescheduling is permitted without penalty if done at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.
