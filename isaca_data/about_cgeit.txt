CGEIT — Certified in the Governance of Enterprise IT
Source: https://www.isaca.org/credentialing/cgeit

CGEIT recognizes a wide range of professionals for their knowledge and application of enterprise IT governance principles and practices. It is designed for experienced professionals who manage, serve in an advisory or oversight role, or otherwise support governance of IT in an organization.

Exam Domains
•	Governance of Enterprise IT
•	IT Resources
•	Benefits Realization
•	Risk Optimization

Certification Requirements
•	Pass the CGEIT certification exam
•	Pay the US$50 application processing fee
•	Submit application demonstrating required work experience (5 years of work experience in the governance of enterprise IT, including at least 1 year in a defining or managing role related to governance frameworks)
•	Adhere to the Code of Professional Ethics
•	Adhere to the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) Policy
Candidates have five years from passing the exam to apply for certification.

Exam Registration
Exam Cost
•	Member: US$575.00
•	Non-Member: US$760.00

Registration Process
CGEIT exams are computer-based and administered at authorized PSI testing centers globally or as remotely proctored exams. Registration is continuous — candidates can register any time with no restrictions. Candidates can schedule a testing appointment as early as 48 hours after payment.
1.	Log in to your ISACA Account
2.	Click Certification & CPE Management
3.	Click Schedule Your Exam or Visit Exam Website (PSI dashboard)
4.	On the PSI dashboard, click Schedule Exam

Eligibility
Upon registration, candidates have a six-month eligibility period to take the exam. Exam appointments are available up to 90 days in advance. Rescheduling is permitted without penalty if done at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.

Why Certify with CGEIT?
•	Demonstrates ability to govern and manage IT at an enterprise level
•	Validates strategic alignment of IT with business goals
•	Ideal for CIOs, IT directors, senior managers, and governance consultants
•	Recognized by organizations seeking strong IT governance leadership
•	Proves understanding of frameworks like COBIT in practice
•	ANAB accredited for internationally recognized credibility

ANAB Accreditation
CGEIT is ANAB (ANSI National Accreditation Board) accredited, providing an internationally recognized mark of credibility and quality.
